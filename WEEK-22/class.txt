Types of Access Modifiers

Public
Private
Protected
Internal
Protected Internal


Public Access Modifiers are accessible from anywhere in the program.

Private Access Modifiers are accessible only within the class.

Protected Access Modifiers are accessible within the class and derived class.

Internal Access Modifiers are accessible within the assembly.

Protected Internal Access Modifiers are accessible within the assembly and derived class.
